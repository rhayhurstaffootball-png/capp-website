What's Included

Every Tool You Need

CAPP is a complete toolkit for Video Coordinators at all levels of football. Every module was built for the pace and precision needed on a daily basis.

Live Scoreboard Entry

Pulls live play-by-play data for CFB and NFL. Auto-fills down, distance, clock, score, and yard line — play by play, in real time.

Voice Entry

Whisper-powered speech recognition for hands-free scoreboard entry. Understands football vocabulary — down, distance, play type, clock — spoken naturally.

Scoreboard Editor

Drag-and-drop layout designer with 18 configurable elements. Build once, reuse every game.

Scoreboard Generator

Renders a 3-second MP4 scoreboard video per play compatible with all major editing systems.

Workflow Manager

Track OFFENSE, DEFENSE, KICKS, and PFF for every opponent on your schedule. WIN/LOSS records, coordinator games, and per-team notes — all in one view.

Clip Splitter (Beta)

Auto-detects scene breaks in your film using motion energy analysis. Converts to a friendly format compatible with all major editing platforms.

Camera Card Tool

Format SD cards in mass for any camera or recorder configuration. Detects card errors, supports multiple formats, and logs every operation for full accountability.

File Distribution

Parallel multi-drive copy with live progress per destination. Built for post-game duplication of full film packages.

Data Converter

Conversion of play data from any source into CAPP formats to generate video scoreboards.

CAPP Nodes

Remote access to any registered machine — live screen share built directly into the launcher. No open ports, no third-party software. Coordinates and GAs stay connected from anywhere.
